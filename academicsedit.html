Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to facilitate the provision of education that enhances empowerment and embraces diversity. We focus on a balanced approach that includes:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science and Technology
  • ChiTonga/IsiNdebele
  • Social Science
  • PE and Arts

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in producing self-reliant individuals. Our students engage in various activities to realize their full potential in every aspect of life:

Sports Clubs Choir & Music Robotics Club Fine Arts

School Excursion

Education at Twaabane extends beyond the classroom walls. Our excursions are designed to help learners think in other terms and explore their communities.
