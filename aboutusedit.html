About Twaabane Primary

Nurturing Excellence, Inspiring Futures.

Our Mission

To facilitate the provision of education that enhances empowerment of the communities to
think in other termsand embrace diversity for gender equality.
Twaabane Primary School's priority is to produce individuals who are self-reliant and guided
by the Christian values of faith, love and prophetic voice.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish and adminster a leadind centre of educational excellence in Binga
and beyond through promotion of innovation, real life and personal growth curriculum.
