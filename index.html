MISSION STATEMENT

To facilitate the provision of education that enhances empowerment of the communities to think in other terms and embraces diversity for gender equality and social inclusion.

Twaabane Primary School's priority is to produce individuals who are self-reliant and guided by the Christian values of faith, love, and prophetic voice. The goal is to empower all learners to become responsible God fearing citizens.
