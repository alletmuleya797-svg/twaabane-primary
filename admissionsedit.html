Admissions

Begin your child's journey toward excellence and self-reliance.

Get Started Today

Requirements

To ensure a smooth registration process, please have the following documents ready:

Certified Copy of Birth Certificate
Latest School Progress Report
Two Passport Sized Photographs
Transfer Letter (if applicable)
Immunization / Health Card
Proof of Residence

How to Apply

1

Obtain Form

Collect an application form from the school office or download it from our portal.

2

Submission

Submit the completed form along with the required documents mentioned above.

3

Assessment

Learners will undergo a friendly placement assessment to determine their current level.

4

Enrolment

Once approved, settle the admission fees to secure your child's place!

Important Dates

Open Days: January 1st - Present
Entrance Assessment: January 11th
Registration Deadline: Ongoing Enrollment
